Stock Market Alert System

## What It Does
Automated stock price monitoring system 
that checks 15 stocks against target prices 
and sends Gmail alerts only when conditions 
are genuinely met.

## Results
15 stocks monitored
11 unnecessary notifications blocked
4 genuine alerts delivered

## Alert Types Supported
Above target price
Below target price
Exact target price hit

## Tools Used
n8n.io | Google Sheets | Gmail

## Workflow
[Schedule_Trigger] → [Google sheets] →[AI] → [GMail]

## Key Achievement
Smart filter reduced unnecessary emails 
by 73% — delivering only genuine alerts.
