# RaterAssist — AI Search Quality Evaluator

## What It Does
Automates the manual search result rating 
process performed for 18 months at Cognizant.
AI reads queries and result snippets, rates 
them Good/Partial/Bad and writes reasons 
back to Google Sheets automatically.

## Tools Used
Make.com | Google Sheets | Make AI Toolkit

## Workflow
[Google Sheets] → [AI Rater] → [Google Sheets]

## Key Achievement
18 months of manual work automated in seconds.
AI does not replace workforce — 
people using AI replace those who do not.
